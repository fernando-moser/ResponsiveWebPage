Why JAPAN?

Japan is definitely one of the most interesting places on earth, especially for its amazing, different and rich culture that nowadays it's so celebrated and well-know all over the world. It sounds funny when you think of how small and far away it is Japan! Maybe that might even be one of the explanations of how their culture was able to travel all the way to western countries and captivate the attention and love from lots and lots of people in here. The Japanese culture its not only extremely lovable, but it's often used as a big inspiration for the entertainment industry! After all, there are so many things that you can enjoy about their culture: food, animes, mangas, movies, games, etc.

With all of that in mind, it's really easy to think about why Japan ended up being the chosen one country for this project! There is so much we could talk about it, in a way that even a whole web page might not be big enough for all the things we would love to talk about...
